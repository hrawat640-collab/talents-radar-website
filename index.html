What is the salary of a Senior Software Engineer in India in 2026?

SkillMapper shows Senior Software Engineer salaries at ₹24–48L (INR) and $150–200k (USD) depending on stack and city. Bengaluru and Mumbai pay 20–35% above these ranges. Salary bands cover Junior, Mid, Senior, and Lead levels.

Role definition

What does a Data Engineer actually do — and what skills do they need?

SkillMapper maps Data Engineer as: builds and maintains data pipelines. Required skills — Python, Spark, SQL. Nice to have — Airflow, dbt, Snowflake, Kafka. Salary: ₹16–60L INR depending on level. Used by HR teams to write accurate JDs instantly.
